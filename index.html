About Current Fitness


Who are we?


Individuals of all ages that are conscious of the environment and their health and wish to track the weather at locations that they desire to visit.


What do we do?


Providing an easy and effortless access to weather tracking whilst coordinating personal outdoor fitness objectives. With the ever expanding and condensing world we live in, there are various aspects of the outdoors that individuals are unaware of or unable to locate consistent accurate data, as an example, pollen, or air quality meters.


Why Air Quality Matters?


Air pollution poses a risk on individuals that are spending time outdoors and prolonged exposure to harmful pollutants is considered unhealthy and can cause issues for people with existing breathing issues such as asthma.
